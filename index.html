About Me

As the title suggests, I'm Robert Hickman. Back on July 21st, 1994, I was born in Fort Worth, Texas. My mother raised my two sisters and me by herself. During my childhood, my family moved all around the country. Below is a list of all the places I lived in chronological order.

  • Fort Worth,Texas
  • Aurora, Colorado
  • Justin, Texas
  • Denton, Texas
  • Mountain view, Arkansas
  • Hendersonville, North Carolina
  • St.Augustine, Florida
  • Denver, Colorado
  • Taos, New Mexico

After all of that moving, we've been settled in Portland for the last 11 years. I finished highschool at Reynolds Highschool in Troutdale. After I graduated, I worked for Arby's and Taco bell while going to Mt.Hood Community College. I earned an Associates in General Studies. I eventually got a Job as a dispatcher for RideConnection for a couple of years. I left to work for the Portland Water Bureau as a water meter reader and I'm currently still working there. Epicodus is my first experience with coding. My friend Ellie is a software engineer and perked my interest in the field.
